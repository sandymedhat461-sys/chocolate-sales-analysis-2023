### **Regional Insights**
- **Sharm** and **Al Sharqia** contributed nearly **90M** each, dominating total sales.  
- **Al Sharqia** achieved the highest unit sales, while **Sharm** generated higher revenue per unit.  
- **Giza** recorded the lowest total sales and units sold.  
- **Alex, Cairo, and Gharbia** maintained similar performance levels.

### **Product Analysis**
- **Caramel Stuffed Bars** led in total revenue.  
- **70% Dark Bites** and **Smooth Silky Salty** were high in demand.  
- **Choco Coated Almonds** sold fewer units but generated strong revenue.

### **Monthly Trends**
- **January:** Highest revenue and sales, with fewer orders.  
- **October:** Highest orders but lower total revenue.  
- **November:** Lowest revenue and orders overall.  
- Sales gradually declined across quarters, while order volume rose.

### **Salesperson Performance**
- **Sara Khaled:** Highest total sales (\$24.6M).  
- **Hassan Ahmed:** Most orders, but ranked lower in total sales.  
- **Ahmed Said** and **Abdel Rahman Mohamed:** Lower total sales and fewer orders.
